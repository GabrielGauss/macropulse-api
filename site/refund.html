Refund Policy

14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you're not satisfied with MacroPulse within the first 14 days of your paid subscription, we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked.

How to Request a Refund

Email support@macropulse.live within 14 days of your subscription start date with:

  • The email address on your account
  • Your request for a refund

We will process your refund within 5–10 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method via Paddle.

After 14 Days

After the 14-day window, subscriptions are non-refundable. You may cancel your subscription at any time to prevent future billing — you will retain access until the end of the current billing period.

Cancellation

To cancel your subscription, use the billing portal at macropulse.live/billing/portal or email support. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

Exceptions

We reserve the right to decline refund requests where we believe the Service was abused, including but not limited to bulk data extraction, API key sharing, or violation of our Terms of Service.
