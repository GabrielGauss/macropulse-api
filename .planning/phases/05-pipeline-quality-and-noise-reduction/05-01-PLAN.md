<objective>
Make critical data failures loud: when WALCL, DGS10, DGS2, or VIX is missing or all-NaN after fetch, the pipeline halts immediately, logs the reason, fires an owner alert, and returns `{"status": "halted", "stale_data": True, ...}`. Optional commodity columns (d_gold, d_oil, d_btc, d_eth) are excluded from the feature DataFrame when unavailable — not zero-filled.

Purpose: Silent data failures currently allow the pipeline to produce signals from incomplete inputs. This fix makes the pipeline fail loudly on non-negotiable series and degrade gracefully on optional series.
Output: Modified daily_pipeline.py (halt logic), feature_engineering.py (column exclusion), and three green tests.
</objective>
